About Me

  • I received the Ph.D. degree in signal and information processing from Xiamen University (XMU), China, in Dec. 2023, advised by Prof. Xinghao Ding at SmartDSP Lab. I was a visiting Ph.D. student with Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, from Dec. 2021 to Dec. 2022, advised by Prof. Kai-Kuang Ma. My current research interests include low-level vision, multimedia information processing, and deep learning.
